Respite's first release. Vanilla's bed is a skip button; Respite makes the night happen — sleeping accelerates time while the world keeps running, a hearty supper heals you overnight, and phantoms trade their insomnia grudge for a real territory. Every feature toggles on its own, and outside the night the world you know is untouched.

## Added
- **Continuous time-lapse** — sleeping accelerates time up to 60× instead of jumping to morning. Furnaces smelt, crops grow, and the stars streak overhead while you sleep. On a server every sleeper adds an equal share of the speed, an AFK sleeper drops out of the share, and a quiet chat whisper names who climbs into or out of bed with the running tally.
- **Restful Saturation** — go to bed on a full hunger bar and your saturation converts into healing overnight: every 2 points of saturation restore 1 heart by morning. Sleep runs deeper the darker the moon, up to double healing on the new moon.
- **Phantoms of the Heights** — insomnia is gone. Phantoms spawn by territory instead: the high sky above Y=100 on any night, and everywhere under a new moon. `doInsomnia` stays the master phantom kill-switch.
- **Weariness** — three in-game days without sleep slows your natural healing until you rest, shown as an ordinary status-effect icon and never a spawned monster. Wake from a full night in bed and you gain **Well-Rested** instead, healing 50% faster for two minutes.
- **The Chronometer** — a copper-and-redstone timepiece block that emits a redstone signal tracking the time of day, one level per 80 seconds. A comparator off the same block reads moon fullness. Sneak-right-click to set an alarm hour and it rings a bell once when it arrives. Ring a clock with copper to make a **pocket chronometer** whose tooltip reads the hour, the moon's phase, and your own days awake.
- **The Caffeinated Brew** — steep cocoa beans and leaves in a water bottle, cook it over a campfire, and drink to clear Weariness and gain 90 seconds of Haste. One drink, not a potion system — the brewing stand is untouched.
- **The Bedroll** — craft one from string and wool, right-click the ground to unroll it and turn in, and it rolls back into your inventory when you wake. It sleeps like a bed for the night but never sets your spawn, and overnight healing runs at half a real bed's rate.
- **Commands** — `/respite status` reports the time-lapse rate, your time awake, Weary state, and nights until the new moon; operator commands cover config reload and rest-state testing.
- **Config** — every feature is independently tunable through `config/respite.json`, hot-reloadable with `/respite reload`, with an in-game settings screen when Mod Menu and Cloth Config are installed.
- **Integrations** — Chronometer clock time and signal show at a glance in Jade and WTHIT when either is present.
